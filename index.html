There are no apps on this word processor, just a dreamy mechanical keyboard and an e-ink screen

35 years after the birth of the laptop computer and 25 years after Wi-Fi was invented, someone finally may have built the ultimate word processor. The Freewrite, which was called the Hemingwrite when it was introduced to the world in a very successful Kickstarter campaign back in 2014, is a portable mechanical keyboard with a small e-ink display on it.
